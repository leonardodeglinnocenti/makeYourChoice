Instructions

1. Click on the "Create a new survey" button to create a new survey. Remember that you need to create the category first (if it's not already present).

2. Click on the "Manage surveys" button to manage your surveys.

3. Click on the "Manage categories" button to manage your categories.

Security measures

1. You cannot force an action by trying to access a page that you are not allowed to access. For example, if you are not logged in or you aren't the owner of a survey, you cannot access the "Delete Survey" page, by passing the survey as a parameter in the browser url bar.

2. The Secret Key is passed as an environment variable, so it's not visible in the code source.

3. Before a critical action is performed, a confirmation message is displayed to the user.
